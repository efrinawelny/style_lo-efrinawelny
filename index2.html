About the Bag

A bag is a container that has a handle and is designed to be easy to carry. Bags have been used by humans since prehistoric times, prehistoric bags were made of animal skins, cotton, or woven plant fibers, which were folded at the edges and tied with ropes of the same material.

It is known that the first bag that can be identified by scientists is estimated to have existed since 2,200-2,500 BC in Profen, Leipzig, Germany. Bags made by prehistoric people used animal skins and bark as the main ingredients.

At present, bags also experience various additional functions with different models such as SlingBag, Backpack, ToteBag and others.
